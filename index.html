Projects

AUDL Predictive Model and Analytics

The American Ultimate Disc Leauge (or AUDL) publishes team and player statistics on their website. For this project, after data exploration, I trained several machine learning algorithms to predict outcomes while tracking thier performance metrics. I then deployed this models using flask

Demonstrates Knowledge of: Python, Flask, Pandas, Numpy, Sklearn, and graphing libraries (Matplotlib/ Seaborn/ Plotly/ Cufflinks)

College Thesis: An Analysis of Urban Renewal and Culutral Heritage Retention

Done for completion of my Sociology major at Whitman College. This study takes a statistical approach, utilizing census data over the past 25 years to analyze how the Five Points neighborhood in Denver, a historic Jazz district , has developed while attempting to maintain a cultural ethos concurrently.

Demonstrates Knowledge of: Statistical practices, presentation of findings, making sense of complex data.
